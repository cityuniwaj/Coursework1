Description

For the first half of my year 13 break, me my mum and my brother went to Canada. We stayed at my uncles house for a month and went sightseeing on a daily basis. Some of the most memorable places we went to were Niagara Falls and Canada's Wonderland. At Niagra Falls we got to go on a boat ride and got very close up to the actual waterfall, we also stayed for the fireworks night show. At Canada's Wonderland, we went on some of the fastest roller coasters in the world such as Leviathan, Behemoth, Yukon Striker and Wildebeast. We also went to my uncles wedding where we had lots of food and had performances to watch while eating. On days that we werent out sightseeing, we stayed at home and watched horror movies and played games on the xbox.

Canada's Wonderland

Canada's Wonderland is an amusement park located in Vaughan, Ontario. It opened in 1981, was the first major theme park in Canada and is still the country's largest at 330 acres. The park has 200 attrations including 17 roller coasters, 2 two children's areas and a water park called Splash Works. Out of all the roller coasters, the most famous ones are Leviathan, being the 8th fastest coaster in the world, and Yukon Striker, featuring a 90 degree free fall drop.

Niagra Falls

Niagara Falls is a group of 3 waterfalls, Horseshoe Falls, American falls and Bridal Veil Falls at southern province of Ontarioin Canada and the state of New York in the United States. It was formed 12000 years ago by the Niagara River, which drains lake Erie into Lake Ontario. The combined falls have the highest flow rate of any waterfalls in North America that has a vertical drop of more than 50m. Druingpeak daytime tourist hours, more than 168,000 cubic metres of water goes over the falls every minute.
